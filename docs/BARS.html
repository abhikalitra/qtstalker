BARS - OHLC Bars

This is the standard OHLC bars plot. With optional color and MA plot overlays.

BARS

INDICATOR,PLUGIN,BARS,<NAME>,<UP COLOR>,<DOWN COLOR>,<NEUTRAL COLOR>

  • <NAME> is the variable name given to the plot
  • <UP COLOR> is the desired color for up bars (higher close than previous bar)
  • <DOWN COLOR> is the desired color for down bars (lower close than previous bar)
  • <NEUTRAL COLOR> is the desired color for neutral bars (no change from previous bar)
