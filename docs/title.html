Title bar

The title bar displays info on the current chart. There is 2 versions.

This is the format for a single chart.

  1. Title of the chart. eg "NASDAQ"
  2. In brackets is the ticker or symbol of the chart. eg (^IXIC)
  3. The current compression format. eg "Daily Compression"

This is the format for a chart that is part of a group.

  1. In square brackets, the group name. eg [Stock Index]
  2. The title of the chart. eg "NASDAQ"
  3. In brackets the ticker or symbol of the chart. eg (^IXIC)
  4. The current compression format. eg "Daily Compression"
