Accumulation Distribution - AD


 

Parameters:

  • Color - AD plot color
  • Line Type - The type of plot to draw
  • Label - The text that identifies the AD plot
  • Method - The type of AD method to use:
    • AD - Chaikin's AD
    • WAD - William's AD
    • CO - Chaikin's Oscillator
    • CMF - Chaikin's Money Flow
  • Period - The period is only used by the CMF method.

Description:


Formula Reference:

Syntax
 AD(Method, Period)
Function
 Method is one of the AD Methods available. Period is used only by the CMF method.
Example
 AD(AD) or AD(CMF, 10)
