Indicator: kk: Dark Cloud Cover (Kabuse) and Piercing Line (Kirikomi)

This document describes the example CUS indicator at misc/CUS_examples/Contributions/xley/kk

A green candle indicates a Piercing Line (Kirikomi). A red candle indicates a Dark Cloud Cover (Kabuse). Other candles are grey.

See also the companion scanner s-kk.
